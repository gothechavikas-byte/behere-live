Information We Collect

1. Google Account Information

When you sign in with Google, we may collect:

  • Your name

We never collect or store your Google password.

2. App Data

We securely store information that you create while using the app, including:

  • Daily streaks

This information is securely stored using Google Firebase Firestore.

3. Device Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device model

  • App version
  • Crash reports
  • Diagnostic information

Crash reports are collected using Firebase Crashlytics solely to improve app stability.
